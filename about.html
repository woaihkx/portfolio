I am Liyuan He.

A proud teacher

Ten years ago, I chose the Teacher Education Program with the mission and expectations of learning advanced education philosophy and spreading Chinese culture. Since then, I have taught Mandarin at a public High School in the Bay Area. Being a reflective world languages educator, I am an active listener who gathers student feedback and incorporates it into my student-centered curricula. My professional experience has demonstrated that being empathetic is required to build trust with students, having a humorous and well-structured presentation style engages them, and being present and available helps them pursue language education throughout their high school careers.

Why a career change?

My experience teaching Chinese in the past 10 years has exposed me to many learning technologies that were clearly not designed to be intuitive or user-friendly. As an empathetic thinker and lifelong educator, I decided to learn UX and apply my skills as an educator to design better tech for the education community.

Ready to contribute as a UX UI designer.

To achieve my dream of working as a better learning experience designer, I have received intensive training through the Bloc UX design program, where I had the opportunity to collaborate with senior designers to complete independent projects.
